Large retailers face staff errors on a daily basis. With many locations, they need a clear system to set up and monitor all processes.

Qvalon develops solutions that structure workflows and centralize task management across large retail networks. This helps minimize staff errors. The primary clients for this service are chain retailers: supermarkets, gas stations, and banks.
